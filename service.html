Electric Motor Services

We provide a wide range of Electric Motor services, with expertise in motors up to 550KW and 2-8 poles. Our services cover squirrel cage and synchronous motors, slipring induction, stator/rotor rewinds, voltage/speed changes, and core repairs/replacement.

Mechanical modifications (bearings, t-boxes, coolers, base plates)

Rotor re-barring (various alloys)

Rotor End-ring replacement

Slip-ring & brushgear repair/replacement

Electric Motor Testing Services

At our facility, we offer comprehensive Electric Motor Testing services with a focus on accuracy and reliability. Our state-of-the-art equipment allows for both static and dynamic testing, providing essential data for motor analysis.

Static Tests - We employ modern equipment for static motor analysis, including winding resistance, insulation resistance, surge analysis, and partial discharge on surge.

Dynamic Tests - Our test bay can handle motors ranging from fractional KW up to 500KW with no load. We provide data on voltage, amps, KW, RPM, torque, and insulation resistance.

Calibrated Equipment - All our testing equipment is calibrated annually to ensure accuracy, and we capture reports digitally during tests.
